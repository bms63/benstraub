Hi all! Great news! We have logrx up on CRAN. The goal of logrx is to facilitate logging in a clinical environment with the goal of making code easily traceable and reproducible. This forms one part of a burgeoning end to end pipeline for doing Clinical Reporting with R.

Please check out the pharmaverse if you are interested in seeing packages for creating ADaM datasets, TLFs or using metadata to drive your analysis or helping to prepare your R work for submission to a regulatory agency.

logrx was formally known as timber, but we had to change it as CRAN already had a package called timber!
